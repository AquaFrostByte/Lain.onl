CueCue
Its a simple bash script combining Cue files into one single disk for PSX using m3u. This works even if all your Roms are in one folder. The Roms have to be in a specific naming order: "MyRom (Disc 1).chd" "MyRom (Disc 2).chd" "MyRom (Disc X).chd" Then the Script will just auto detect them if your roms are named diffrently feel free to change the sctipt
